The sand cat (Felis margarita) is a small wild cat that inhabits sandy and stony deserts far from water sources

The sand cat primarily resides in the sahara and arabian deserts, the cats paws are heavily furred, leaving practically no footprints in the desert, the cat is incredibly quiet and solitary. The sand cat has no need to drink water, as it absorbs enough liquid from animal blood.
